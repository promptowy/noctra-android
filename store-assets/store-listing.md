## FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars)

Noctra is a browser that blocks ads and trackers by default — no setup, no toggles, no subscriptions.

Open it and browse. That's it.

**What Noctra blocks:**
→ Ads on every website
→ Tracking scripts (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
→ Cookie consent popups (OneTrust, CookieBot, Didomi)
→ Sponsored content injections

**Features:**
★ Built-in ad blocker — 98,000+ blocked domains (EasyList + EasyPrivacy)
★ Cookie wall remover — hides GDPR popups automatically
★ Bookmarks — tap ★ to save, accessible from the menu
★ Multiple profiles — separate browsing sessions with color-coded tabs
★ Shield stats — tap the green chip to see what was blocked on this page
★ Disable shield per site — one tap to whitelist a site
★ Restore closed tab — accidentally closed something? Menu → Restore
★ Clean user agent — no "wv" WebView fingerprint

**Privacy:**
Noctra collects zero data. No analytics. No crash reports. No ads inside the browser. Everything stays on your device.

Blocklist source: EasyList, EasyPrivacy (open source, bundled locally).
